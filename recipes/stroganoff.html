Brazilian Stroganoff

Description

Brazilian Stroganoff is a delicious variant of a traditional beef stroganoff (usually made with noodles), that uses tomato paste and crema de leite, chicken, and is served on rice. Brazilian Stroganoff is often topped with shoestring potatoes, as all good Brazilian dishes are!

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Chicken cut into cubes
  • Diced onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Crema de leite
  • White rice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a large pot or pan, start by heating the olive oil and cooking the diced onions and garlic until they are soft.
  2. Next, add the cubed chicken and cook through.
  3. While the chicken is cooking, start the rice in a separate pot, or use a rice cooker.
  4. Add the crema de leite and tomato paste, and mix with the chicken until it is a consistent mixture.
  5. Ensure the mixture is warmed through, and add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. To serve, add rice to the plate first, then add the stroganoff on top with a ladle. Top it off with some shoestring potatoes, and you're ready to go!
